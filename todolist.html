Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favarite foods. here is a recipe for deliciosly rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a ftrying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatule fold the edge of the pan to the center envery 20 sconds (as if you are marking an oneleette)
  5. When the eggs are still mist remove from the heat (it will continue to cock on the plate until served)
